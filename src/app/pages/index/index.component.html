Welcome to the
E-Fest 2k20!

This is the 3rd edition of one of the best "Peda" trips of the year.
Possible dates:
May 23th - 25th or June 6th - 8th

This festival was created with the intention of enjoying together with colleagues and friends, because of their qualities and great value as people as well as their performance in the "peda", were considered to be part of this new edition.

We are interested in the fact that most of the guests can enjoy this Fest, that is why we collaborate with everyone in order to make the trip fit our budgets as much as possible. Enjoying is the main thing, those contemplated for this edition were carefully selected for the quality of friendship that they can offer, their skills to socialize,drinking performance and to keep hidden and controlled the “malacopa” we carry inside.

Game Changing!

Previous E-Fest were made in tourist destinations such as Guanajuato Capital and Mazamitla, taking advantage of all the potential that each city could offer us, for this year we are going to try another style, start preparing mojitos & mimosas and renewing your swimsuit. We're going to the beach!.
